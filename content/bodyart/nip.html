The horizontal piercing was done at 10ga by Patrick of the London Piercing Clinic and the vertical was done at 14ga by Cushla of Into You. The horizontal piercing was the only piercing I've had done under anaesthetic and it healed completely in under two months. The vertical piercing was done about 6 months ago without anaesthetic and has been been reasonably trouble free but has definitely taken longer to heal. The ring was originally at the base of the nipple but I seem to get a lot of migration as you can see - although the bar hasn't moved much at all.

In case you're wondering, it's the ring that's crooked - it was perfectly level when it was done but has migrated both forwards and to an angle.
